Attractions in Macon County

Explore attractions, city center, and activities.

  • Macon County offers a variety of natural attractions, outdoor activities, and scenic destinations for visitors.

    Main Attractions:

    • Outdoor recreation including hiking, fishing, and camping
    • Mountain views and scenic overlooks
    • Local parks and recreational areas
    • Rivers, lakes, and waterfalls

    Popular Destinations:

    • Nantahala National Forest
    • Wayah Bald and observation tower
    • Dry Falls and Bridal Veil Falls
    • Little Tennessee River

    Macon County provides year-round opportunities for outdoor recreation and tourism.
